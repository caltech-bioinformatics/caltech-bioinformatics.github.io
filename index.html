NGS bioinformatics is an interdisciplinary field that integrates computer science, mathematics, statistics, software engineering to tackle complex biological questions. CBRC provides pipeline development, data analysis and hands-on workshops to both Caltech and non-Caltech customers.

Statistical analysis is essential for designing biological experiments. Weak statistical standards contribute to irreproducbility of biological results. CBRC provides experimental design & power analysis, statistical analysis and lectures to both Caltech and non-Caltech customers.
